# Release 0.1 — Landing Experience
## Felipe Santander Platform

Version: 0.1
Status: Approved
Depends on:
- Brand Constitution v1.0
- Product Constitution v1.0

---

# Goal

Build and publish the first public version of the platform.

The objective is not a complete portfolio.

The objective is to create a premium landing experience that immediately communicates positioning, credibility and strategic value.

---

# Success Criteria

A recruiter should understand within 60 seconds:

- Who Felipe is.
- What problems he solves.
- Why he is different.
- Why it is worth continuing the conversation.

---

# Scope

Create ONLY the Home page.

Do not implement internal pages yet.

Reserve the following future routes:

/about
/transformations
/playbook
/admin

---

# Information Architecture

1. Hero
2. The Problem
3. My Approach
4. Transformations
