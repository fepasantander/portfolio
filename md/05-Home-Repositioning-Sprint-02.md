# 05-Home-Repositioning-Sprint-02
Version: 1.0

# Objective

Refactor the current Home page.

Do NOT redesign from scratch.

Increase perceived executive value while preserving the existing architecture.

---

# Primary Goal

The visitor must conclude:

"I am not looking at a UX portfolio.

I am looking at someone capable of transforming how companies build digital products."

---

# Keep

- Visual identity
- Tech stack
- Existing architecture
- Responsive behavior
- Design System

---

# Reorder Home

1. Hero
2. Organizational Problem
3. How I Think
4. Why Companies Hire Me
5. Transformations
6. Testimonials
7. About Me
8. CTA

---

# Hero

Do not introduce Felipe first.

Introduce the business problem first.

Felipe appears as the consequence.

---

# New Section: How I Think

Create four premium cards.

1. Research before opinion.

2. Strategy before interface.

3. Engineering from day one.

4. AI amplifies intelligence. It does not replace it.

Each card:
- short title
- one paragraph
- elegant icon
- subtle hover

---

# New Section: Why Companies Hire Me

Do not list skills.

Explain capabilities.

Use six capability blocks:

- Connect Product, Business and Engineering.
- Increase UX maturity.
- Build scalable product foundations.
- Reduce friction between Design and Development.
- Structure AI-enabled products responsibly.
- Transform complexity into clear execution.
